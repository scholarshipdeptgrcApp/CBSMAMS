About Us

The Motortrade Life and Livelihood Assistance Foundation, Inc. (MLALAF) was registered with Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as a non-stock, non-profit organization on April 25, 2001 by a group of incorporators who are interested in the improvement of the quality of life and the social and spiritual values of the urban poor. It is a family foundation. Its main office is located at TPI Bldg., EDSA corner Harvard St., Brgy Pinagkaisahan, Makati City.

Global Reciprocal Colleges (GRC) is a private college located on 9th Avenue, Caloocan City, Metro Manila, Philippines. It was founded in 2008 by Vicente N. Ongtenco, a businessman whose family also owns Motortrade, a well-known motorcycle company in the Philippines. Ongtenco is also the chairman of the college, and he is assisted by his children in managing it. He established the school to help underprivileged students gain access to higher education by providing scholarships and financial assistance to deserving students. As of 2017, the college had between 3,500 to 4,000 students, with around 30% of them being scholars.

We Offer 100% Scholarship!

REQUIREMENTS

  • Current grades; Form 138 (if freshman); T.O.R. (if transferee); Latest grade slip (if GRC student already); Current Certificate of Matriculation (if GRC student already);
  • GRC Admission slip or proof of successful compliance with all the GRC Admission requirements (if freshman or transferred-in);
  • Two (2) Recommendation Letter (from two (2) of any of the ff: Teacher, principal, guidance counselor ,pastor, and/or public official);
  • Testimony (A 500-word essay of the applicant’s life: story, family situation, and his/her reasons why he/she should be granted a scholarship.)
  • House Photos (must be 1 page only) — Photos of applicant’s house, which should cover the ff:
    1. Outside: Front, Back, Sides (Left and Right)
    2. Inside: Living Area, Dining, Kitchen, CR and Bedroom/s; and
    3. If it’s possible, include the photos of the houses in the surrounding area.

QUALIFICATIONS

  • Shall be a Filipino citizen;
  • Senior High School graduate, transferred-in student, or currently enrolled in GRC,
  • have successfully passed the entrance examinations of GRC;
  • an indigent, family’s total annual gross income shall not be exceeding to One Hundred Fifty-Six Thousand Pesos (₱156,000.00);
  • shall not presently be availing any scholarship or student grant from CHED, any government offices, NGOs, politicians, or another institution;
  • shall have no INC., F.A, 5.00, or any grade remarks equivalent to these on the recent grade report, and
  • a freshman or sophomore college student in GRC.
